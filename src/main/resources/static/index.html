Welcome to CatScout!

Finding the purrfect companion can be a daunting task. With so many options for places to adopt from, it can be really scary! Our mission is to alleviate that stress and make the process of meeting your furever friend more accessible so more kitties can find homes. On our site, you can filter through nearby pet adoption sites and save cats for later.

Don't know where to start? Check out our New Owners page, where we answer FAQ and condense need-to-know information in a bite size, easy to reference format! It's important to go into pet ownership knowing the proper facts so you and your fur-ever friend can thrive! It's no fun going in blind for both you and your new companion!
